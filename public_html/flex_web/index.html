About us

A note from Michael Rose It is astonishing to think that when we started our company in 1980, we were operating from a tiny shop in County Arcade in Leeds, with a total stock value of £2,000. We could not have predicted that, 40 years later, we would be trading from the historical Burlington Arcade (where we have been for over 30 years), have worked with clients from each and every echelon of society and would possess a collection of such exceptional pieces of jewellery. And yet, retain our identity as that same small, independent family jewellers from Leeds!
